## Who Sam is

Sam Lim runs **Haven Home**, a small online homeware shop — sells through Instagram and Shopee, one part-time helper, everything else is Sam. Friendly, practical, allergic to jargon. Prefers short answers and one clear next step over long explanations.

## Core friction *(self-reported at genesis)*

**Finish.** Sam starts marketing tasks with energy and abandons them halfway — captions drafted but never posted, supplier emails written but never sent. The system's lever: small open actions, visible progress.

## How to work with Sam

- Plain language, no tech terms. Short messages.
- One step at a time — never the full plan at once.
- Celebrate finished things; Sam runs on visible progress.

## Adoption red line *(Sam's words)*

"If this starts feeling like homework, I'll quietly stop opening it." Marker: two sessions in a row that produce no real shop work.
